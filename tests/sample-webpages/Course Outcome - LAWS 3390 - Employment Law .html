Course Outcome


LAWS 3390 - Employment Law

Learning Outcome
 

Students will obtain knowledge of the specific and unique elements of contract (and, to a lesser extent, tort) that apply to the employment relationship, including: government regulation of the employment relationship; anti-discrimination provisions and how they are applied by the courts; how the Labour Department and the Labour Tribunal regulate the employment of the low paid and/or marginalised and arbitrate any disputes. All the important specific provisions resulting from these matters will be studied in depth.
